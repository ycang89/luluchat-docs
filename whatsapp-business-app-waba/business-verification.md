# Business Verification

## What is Business Verification?

Business Verification is the process of proving to Meta that your business is legitimate and compliant with WhatsApp Business policies. Completing this verification unlocks important benefits, including the blue checkmark (verified badge) and higher messaging tier limits.

## Why Get Verified?

### Tier Progression Benefits

**Faster Tier Advancement**:
- Verified businesses often progress through messaging tiers more quickly
- Tier upgrades are reviewed more favorably for verified accounts
- Higher initial limits may be granted to verified businesses

**Higher Messaging Limits**:
- Access to Tier 3 (100,000 conversations/month) is typically easier for verified businesses
- Verified accounts may receive custom limits (Tier 4) more readily
- Better rate limits for sending messages

**Account Standing**:
- Verified status demonstrates legitimacy to Meta
- Better protection against account restrictions
- Priority consideration for limit increases

### Additional Benefits

- **Trust & Credibility**: Blue checkmark shows customers your business is verified
- **Brand Recognition**: Your verified business name appears in conversations
- **Priority Support**: Access to better support from Meta
- **Feature Access**: Some advanced features may be available only to verified businesses

## Verification Requirements

### Prerequisites

Before you can apply for business verification, you need:

- [ ] **Active WABA Account**: Your WhatsApp Business App account must be active and connected
- [ ] **Complete Business Profile**: All required business information in Meta Business Manager
- [ ] **Business Registration**: Legitimate, registered business entity
- [ ] **Valid Documentation**: Current business registration documents
- [ ] **Compliance History**: No policy violations on your account
- [ ] **Active Messaging**: Demonstrated legitimate business use of WhatsApp

### Required Information

**Business Details**:
- Legal business name (must match registration documents)
- Business address
- Contact information
- Business type/industry
- Tax identification number (where applicable)

**Documentation**:
- Business registration certificate
- Tax registration documents
- Proof of business address (utility bills, lease agreements)
- Government-issued business license (if required in your region)
- Additional documents as requested by Meta

**Account Requirements**:
- Complete Meta Business Manager profile
- Active WABA account with messaging history
- Compliance with WhatsApp Business policies
- Positive quality rating

## How Verification Helps with Tier Progression

### Tier 1 to Tier 2

**Without Verification**:
- Requires 30+ days of positive messaging history
- Consistent high quality scores
- No policy violations
- Gradual progression

**With Verification**:
- Faster review process
- More favorable consideration
- May skip some waiting periods
- Higher likelihood of approval

### Tier 2 to Tier 3

**Without Verification**:
- Requires extended positive history (60+ days)
- Excellent quality scores consistently
- Very high engagement metrics
- May take several months

**With Verification**:
- Tier 3 access is more accessible
- Verification demonstrates legitimacy
- Faster progression timeline
- Higher success rate for upgrades

### Tier 3 to Tier 4 (Custom Limits)

**Without Verification**:
- Very difficult to achieve
- Requires exceptional metrics
- Long history of excellence
- May require direct Meta relationship

**With Verification**:
- Custom limits more achievable
- Meta more willing to negotiate
- Priority consideration
- Better support for limit requests

{% hint style="info" %}
**Key Insight**: Business verification doesn't automatically upgrade your tier, but it significantly improves your chances and speeds up the process. Combined with high quality messaging, verification is one of the best ways to unlock higher tiers.
{% endhint %}

## Maintaining Verified Status

### Ongoing Requirements

Once verified, you must maintain:

- **Policy Compliance**: Continue following WhatsApp Business policies
- **Quality Standards**: Maintain high message quality ratings
- **Active Account**: Keep your WABA account active and in good standing
- **Updated Information**: Keep business information current in Meta Business Manager

### What Can Cause Loss of Verification

- **Policy Violations**: Serious or repeated violations
- **Quality Issues**: Consistently low quality ratings
- **Account Restrictions**: Multiple account restrictions or warnings
- **Inactive Account**: Extended period of inactivity
- **Document Expiration**: Business documents expire and aren't updated

{% hint style="warning" %}
**Important**: Losing verification can also result in tier reduction or limit restrictions. Always maintain compliance and quality to protect your verified status.
{% endhint %}

## Important behavior to know

- **Not Guaranteed**: Verification is not automatic - Meta reviews each application
- **Takes Time**: The process typically takes 2-4 weeks, sometimes longer
- **Can Be Rejected**: Not all businesses will be approved
- **Ongoing Requirement**: You must maintain compliance to keep verified status
- **Tier Not Automatic**: Verification helps with tiers but doesn't guarantee upgrades
- **Regional Variations**: Requirements and process may vary by country

## Common issues & solutions

- **Application Rejected**:
  - Review rejection reason in Meta Business Manager
  - Ensure all documents match your business information exactly
  - Check for any policy violations on your account
  - Update incomplete business profile information
  - Wait 30 days before reapplying

- **Long Review Time**:
  - Normal review takes 2-4 weeks
  - High application volumes can cause delays
  - Meta may request additional information (extends timeline)
  - Be patient and monitor your status regularly

- **Missing Documents**:
  - Check Meta's requirements for your country/region
  - Ensure documents are current and valid
  - Documents must match business information exactly
  - Contact Meta support if you're unsure what's needed

- **Tier Not Upgrading After Verification**:
  - Verification helps but doesn't guarantee tier upgrades
  - Continue maintaining high quality messaging
  - Tier upgrades are reviewed separately
  - May take additional time after verification approval
