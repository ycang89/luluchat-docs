# Blue Tick (Verified Business)

## What is the Blue Tick?

The blue checkmark (âœ“) next to your business name indicates that your business is **verified by Meta**. This shows customers that your business is authentic, trustworthy, and officially recognized by WhatsApp.

## Benefits of Verification

- **Trust**: Customers see you're a verified business, increasing confidence in your brand
- **Brand Recognition**: Your verified business name appears prominently in conversations
- **Higher Limits**: Verified businesses often have higher messaging limits and rate limits
- **Priority Support**: Access to better support from Meta and faster issue resolution
- **Professional Appearance**: The blue tick adds credibility to your business communications
- **Feature Access**: Some advanced features may be available only to verified businesses

## How to Get Verified

### Step 1: Complete Business Verification

Submit your business information to Meta through Meta Business Manager. This includes:
- Business name and legal entity information
- Business address and contact details
- Business registration documents
- Tax identification numbers (where applicable)

### Step 2: Meet Requirements

Your business must meet these requirements:
- **Legitimate Business Entity**: You must be a registered, legitimate business
- **Complete Business Profile**: All required business information must be provided
- **Compliance**: Your business must comply with WhatsApp Business policies
- **Active WABA Account**: You must have an active WhatsApp Business App account
- **Valid Documentation**: All submitted documents must be valid and current

### Step 3: Review Process

Meta reviews your application, which can take:
- **Initial Review**: Several days to a few weeks
- **Additional Information**: Meta may request additional documentation
- **Final Decision**: Typically takes 2-4 weeks, but can take longer

### Step 4: Approval

Once approved:
- The blue tick appears automatically next to your business name
- Your verified status is visible to all customers
- You gain access to verified business benefits

{% hint style="info" %}
**Note**: Verification is not automatic. You must apply through Meta Business Manager and meet their requirements. The process can take time and is not guaranteed. Not all businesses will be approved.
{% endhint %}

## Important behavior to know

- **Not Guaranteed**: Verification is not automatic and not all businesses will be approved
- **Time Investment**: The verification process can take several weeks
- [ ] **Ongoing Compliance**: You must maintain compliance to keep your verified status
- **Can Be Revoked**: Meta can revoke verification if you violate policies
- **WABA Only**: Verification is only available for WABA accounts, not WhatsApp Personal
- **Regional Variations**: Requirements and availability may vary by country

## Common issues & solutions

- **Verification Not Showing**:
  - Ensure your business verification is complete in Meta Business Manager
  - The process can take several weeks - be patient
  - Check that all required documents were submitted correctly

- **Application Rejected**:
  - Review Meta's business verification requirements
  - Ensure all documents are valid and current
  - Check for any policy violations on your account
  - You can reapply after addressing issues

- **Long Review Time**:
  - Verification reviews typically take 2-4 weeks
  - Meta may request additional information, which extends the timeline
  - High application volumes can cause delays

- **Missing Requirements**:
  - Ensure your business is properly registered
  - Complete all required fields in Meta Business Manager
  - Submit all required documentation
