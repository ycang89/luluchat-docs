# Account Management

Manage your company profile, billing information, integrations, team users, and platform branding.

{% hint style="info" %}
**What You Can Manage**

* **Company Profile**: Update your workspace name, logo, and company details
* **Billing**: View plans, usage, and manage subscriptions
* **Integrations**: Connect external services like Google Calendar, Zapier, and more
* **Team Users**: Invite, manage, and remove team members
* **App Branding**: Customize your workspace appearance
{% endhint %}
