# Tickets

## What is the Tickets tab?

Creates a ticket linked to the contact directly from the contact info panel in Inbox.

## When does it trigger?

* When you add a ticket from the `Tickets` tab while viewing the conversation.

## What happens after it triggers?

A ticket is created and linked to the contact; it appears in Tickets views (summary/board/list).

## Important behavior to know

* Requires Tickets access and valid fields.
* Linkage to the contact is automatic from this tab.

## Common issues & solutions

* Cannot create: confirm permissions and required fields.
* Ticket not visible: check board/list filters or status in Tickets.
