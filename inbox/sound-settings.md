# Sound Settings (Mute/Unmute)

## What are Sound Settings?

Controls to mute or enable inbox sound notifications for new messages.

## When does it trigger?

* When you toggle sound to muted or unmuted in Inbox.

## What happens after it triggers?

New message alerts play or stay silent based on your setting; it applies to your session/user.

## Important behavior to know

* Sound preference is user-specific; it does not affect other teammates.
* Some browsers require notification permission for sounds.

## Common issues & solutions

* No sound after unmuting: check browser permissions, system volume, and channel connectivity.
* Still hearing sounds when muted: refresh the page and confirm the icon shows muted.
