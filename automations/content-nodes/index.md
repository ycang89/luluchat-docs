# Content Nodes

## Overview
Content nodes are the primary way you communicate with your customers. These steps allow you to send text, media, official templates, or even redirect users to other interactive experiences like forms or AI agents.

## Nodes in this Section
- [Message](./message.md): Send standard text and media messages.
- [Message Template](./message-template.md): Use pre-approved official WhatsApp templates.
- [Start Flow](./start-flow.md): Connect different automation flows together.
- [Form](./form.md): Send interactive web forms to collect data.
- [AI Agent](./ai-agent.md): Engage customers with dynamic AI-powered conversations.
