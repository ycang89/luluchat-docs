# Content Nodes

## Overview

Content nodes are the primary way you communicate with your customers. These steps allow you to send text, media, official templates, or even redirect users to other interactive experiences like forms or AI agents.

{% hint style="info" %}
**Communication Methods**

- **Direct Messages**: Send text and media directly to customers
- **Official Templates**: Use pre-approved WhatsApp message templates
- **Interactive Forms**: Collect customer information
- **AI Conversations**: Engage with dynamic AI-powered responses
{% endhint %}
