# Deals

## What is Deals?
The Deals module is designed to help you track your sales opportunities. It allows you to visualize your sales pipeline, track potential revenue, and manage the progress of every lead from initial contact to a closed deal.

## Core Features
- **Sales Pipelines**: Create custom stages that represent your unique sales funnel.
- **Revenue Tracking**: Assign monetary values to deals and see the total value of each stage on the board.
- **Contact Integration**: Link deals directly to your contacts for a complete view of the customer journey.
- **Reporting**: Analyze your sales performance, win rates, and priority distribution.

## Module Structure
- [Summary](./summary.md): Visual reports on your sales performance and pipeline health.
- [Board](./board.md): The visual Kanban interface for managing active sales opportunities.
- [List](./list.md): A detailed table view of all deals in your pipeline.
